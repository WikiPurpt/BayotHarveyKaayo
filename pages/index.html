Bayot Harvey

Help a little, give a lot

Bayot Harvey is a non stock non profit gay organization founded by progressive youth leaders in the city of Mabini with a common vision for youth and community building.

A gay organization with a vision of creating networks, gay community engagement, and social development, ayot Harvey prides itself as a grassroot gay organization dedicated in advancing pro-people agendas. Efforts were them made to legitimize the organization's existence through applying for registration at the Securities and Exchange Commission and was granted approval on July 15, 2022.
